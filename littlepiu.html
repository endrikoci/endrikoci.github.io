Little Piu

Little Piu is an iOS game for iPhone and iPad.

Little Piu is a small chick with a big dream: to fly over the blue sea. Help Little Piu to fly as far as possible and make sure he doesn't fall into the depths of the sea! Draw on the screen elastic ropes which will help him bounce back into the sky. Discover and play with the friends of Little Piu! They have different weights and different impacts with the ropes, all for a diversified gameplay experience.
